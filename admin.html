Step 1 — Approve Players
  1. Open the Players tab.
  2. New registrations show up as pending.
  3. Click ✓ to approve or ✗ to reject.

Only approved players can be placed into groups.

Step 2 — Create Groups
  1. Open the Groups tab. Approved players are auto-split into groups.
  2. Move a player into a different group if you want a specific split.
  3. Click Save Groups & Generate Schedule.

This auto-creates the full round-robin schedule — no need to add matches manually.

Step 3 — Run a Match (Match Day)
  1. Open the Matches tab and pick a match.
  2. Click Set Live. Only 2 matches can be live at once.
  3. Share the Join Code (e.g. GRP-A-M1) with both players.
  4. Tell them: "Go to league.getgol.in → Join tab → enter the code and your name."
  5. After confirming ready, players can submit their manager/team setup — you'll see both submissions right on this match's card.
  6. They play the match in eFootball (one of them creates the 1v1 Match Room and shares that code with the other directly — that's separate from our Join Code).
Scoring — players can self-report

After playing, either player can enter the final score themselves from the My Matches tab on the player site — no need for you to do it. If both players enter the same score, the match completes automatically and standings update — nothing for you to do. If their scores don't match, you'll see a ⚠️ Score mismatch flag on this match's card showing both numbers — check with the players and enter the correct score yourself to resolve it. You can also enter or override any score manually at any time, same as before.

Step 4 — Standings & Knockout

Standings update automatically as scores are entered — no manual calculation. Once every group match is completed, the knockout bracket is generated automatically from each group's top finishers. The Knockout tab works exactly like Matches: set live, share the code, enter the score. Semifinal losers automatically get a 3rd place match. The winner is announced when the Final completes.

Match Reminders

Players can tap "Enable" on their My Matches tab to get a push notification ~10 minutes before their match's scheduled time — this is fully automatic once they opt in, nothing for you to set up per match. It only works if you've set a date and time on the match (the 📅 Schedule button), since that's what the reminder is timed against.

Handling Problems
  • Score mismatch flagged: see the "Scoring" section above — enter the correct score to resolve it.
  • Player disputes a score: review it under the Disputes tab and resolve with a short note.
  • You entered a wrong score: go back to Matches/Results and re-enter it — it overwrites the old one.
  • Every action you take is recorded in the Activity Log tab with a timestamp.
Managing Other Admins

Only the default admin can add or remove admins, from the Admins tab. Maximum 3 admins total. New admins just sign in at league.getgol.in/admin.html with their own Google account — no password to share.
