How eLeague Works

1. Register

Fill in your name and eFootball ID on the Profile tab. Your registration needs admin approval before you're confirmed — check back on the Home tab.

2. Check your schedule

Once approved and your matches are scheduled, they'll appear in your Profile tab below your player card. The Matches tab shows all players' matches sorted by date.

3. Play the match

Play in the eFootball app directly — this website doesn't host the gameplay. One of you creates a 1v1 Match Room in eFootball and shares the room code with your opponent via WhatsApp.

4. Report the score

After playing, go to your Profile tab and enter the final score on your live match card. If you and your opponent both enter the same score, it's confirmed automatically. If scores don't match, the admin will step in to resolve it.

5. Get match reminders

On your Profile tab, tap Enable to get a notification 10 minutes before your scheduled match starts — even if you don't have the app open.

6. Track the tournament

Check Standings for group tables, Leaderboard for top scorers/wins, Results for completed match scores, and Bracket for the knockout stage once it starts.

⚠️ Report an Issue

If a result looks wrong or you have a problem, email the admin directly. Include your name, the match join code, and what happened.
