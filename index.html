How eLeague Works

1. Register

Fill in your name and eFootball ID on the Register tab. Your registration needs admin approval before you're confirmed — check back on the Home tab.

2. Confirm ready & submit your setup

When your match goes live, the admin shares a Join Code (e.g. GRP-A-M1) — enter it on the Join tab with your name to confirm you're ready. You can also submit your manager name, formation, and team there, so the admin can verify it later.

3. Play the match

Play in the eFootball app directly — this website doesn't host the gameplay. One of you creates a 1v1 Match Room in eFootball and shares that code with your opponent directly (WhatsApp, etc.) — that's a different code from our Join Code.

4. Report the score

After playing, go to My Matches, search your name, and enter the final score on your live match. If you and your opponent both enter the same score, it's confirmed automatically. If your scores don't match, you'll see a warning — double check with your opponent and the admin will step in to resolve it.

5. Get match reminders

On the My Matches tab, tap Enable to get a notification 10 minutes before your scheduled match starts — even if you don't have the app open.

6. Track the tournament

Check Standings for group tables, Stats for your goals/wins, and Bracket for the knockout stage once it starts. If a result looks wrong, use the Dispute tab to flag it for admin review.
