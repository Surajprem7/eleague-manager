# Admin Guide — How to Run a Tournament

A simple step-by-step guide for running matches and coordinating players. No technical knowledge needed.

Login: go to **league.getgol.in/admin.html** and sign in with your Google account.

---

## Step 1: Approve Players

1. Open the **Players** tab.
2. New registrations show up here as **pending**.
3. Check each player's name and eFootball ID.
4. Click the **✓ (green tick)** to approve, or **✗ (red cross)** to reject.
5. Repeat until everyone you want in the tournament is approved.

> Only approved players can be placed into groups.

---

## Step 2: Create Groups

1. Open the **Groups** tab.
2. All approved players appear here, already split into groups (Group A, B, C...).
3. You can drag/move a player into a different group if you want a specific split.
4. When happy with the groups, click **Save Groups & Generate Schedule**.

> This automatically creates the full round-robin schedule — every player in a group plays every other player in that group once. You don't need to create matches manually.

---

## Step 3: Run a Match (Match Day)

1. Open the **Matches** tab. You'll see the full schedule, grouped by phase.
2. Pick the match you want to start.
3. Click **Set Live** on that match.
   - **Only 2 matches can be live at the same time.** If you try a 3rd, the app will block it — finish or wait for one of the current live matches before starting another.
4. Once a match is live, share the **Join Code** (e.g. `GRP-A-M1`) with both players via WhatsApp/chat.
5. Tell players: *"Open league.getgol.in, go to the Join tab, enter this code and your name to confirm you're ready."*
6. Players play their match in the eFootball app directly (the website doesn't host the actual gameplay).
7. After the match finishes, come back to the **Matches** tab, find that match, and **enter the score** (home goals / away goals).
8. Click **Save**. The match moves to **completed**, standings update automatically, and the next live slot opens up.

---

## Step 4: Watch Standings Update

- Standings (points, wins, draws, losses, goal difference) update automatically as you enter scores — no manual calculation needed.
- Players can check their own group's standings from the public site's **Standings** tab.

---

## Step 5: Knockout Stage

- Once **every group match is completed**, the app automatically generates the knockout bracket (Round of 16 / Quarterfinals / Semifinals / Final, depending on how many groups/players you had) using the top finishers from each group.
- Open the **Knockout** tab — it works exactly like the group stage: set a match live, share the join code, enter the score when done.
- The semifinal losers automatically get a **3rd place match**.
- When the Final is completed, the winner is announced automatically.

---

## Step 6: Handling Problems

- **A player disputes a score** ("the result entered is wrong"): they submit it from the public site's **Dispute** tab. You'll see it under your **Disputes** tab — review it and mark it resolved with a short note on what you decided.
- **You made a mistake entering a score**: go back to the **Matches** or **Results** tab, find the match, and re-enter the correct score — it overwrites the old one.
- **A player needs to be removed mid-tournament**: reject or contact them directly; there's currently no "withdraw" button, so handle name/group reshuffling manually if this comes up (rare).

---

## Step 7: Check What Happened (Activity Log)

- Every action you take (approvals, scores, knockout generation, admin changes) is automatically logged under the **Activity Log** tab, with a timestamp.
- Useful if a player questions something — you can show exactly when and what was done.

---

## Managing Other Admins

- Only the **default admin** (the original account) can add or remove other admins.
- Go to the **Admins** tab → enter a Gmail address → click **Add**. That person just signs in at `league.getgol.in/admin.html` with that Google account — no password to share.
- Maximum of 3 admins total at any time.

---

## Quick Reference — Tab Cheat Sheet

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Players** | Approve/reject registrations |
| **Groups** | Assign players into groups, generate the schedule |
| **Matches** | Set matches live, enter scores |
| **Knockout** | Same as Matches, but for the knockout bracket |
| **Results** | Browse all completed matches |
| **Disputes** | Review and resolve player-raised issues |
| **Activity Log** | See a full history of every admin action |
| **Admins** | Add/remove other admins (default admin only) |
